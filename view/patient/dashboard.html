Tips to be healthy

1)Get enough sleep

Quality sleep is essential for your body to recover and rejuvenate. Aim for 7-9 hours of restful sleep each night. Establish a bedtime routine, create a comfortable sleep environment, and limit screen time before bed to ensure you wake up feeling refreshed.

2)Regular Check-Ups and Preventive Care

Don't skip your regular check-ups with healthcare professionals. These appointments are crucial for early detection of health issues and maintaining your well-being. Stay up-to-date with vaccinations, screenings, and preventive care recommendations.
